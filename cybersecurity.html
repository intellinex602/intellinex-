FAQ

Security Common Questions

What is a Security Operations Center (SOC)?+

A Security Operations Center (SOC) is a facility housing an information security team responsible for monitoring and analyzing an organization's security posture on an ongoing, 24/7 basis. Intellinex provides this enterprise capability as a service, eliminating the massive overhead of building your own internal SOC.

How does Zero-Trust security differ from traditional network models?+

Traditional models rely on "perimeter security" (castle-and-moat) where anyone inside the network is trusted. Zero-Trust operates on the principle of "never trust, always verify," meaning authentication and authorization are continuously validated for every single connection, preventing attackers from moving laterally even if they breach the perimeter.

Can you help us prepare for SOC 2 Type II or ISO 27001 audits?+

Yes, compliance readiness is one of our primary cybersecurity services. We conduct pre-audit assessments, configure your infrastructure to match required controls, implement logging and monitoring frameworks, and provide comprehensive evidence-generation reports for your auditors.
