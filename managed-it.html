FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Managed Service Provider (MSP)?+

An MSP like Intellinex proactively manages and takes responsibility for your IT infrastructure and end-user systems on your behalf. Instead of reactive break-fix IT, we prevent problems before they occur, giving you predictable costs and maximum uptime.

How quickly can you onboard our company?+

Most organizations are fully onboarded within 15-30 days. We conduct an initial audit in week one, deploy our monitoring and management tools in week two, and are fully operational by week three — all with zero disruption to your existing operations.

What does 99.9% uptime SLA mean in practice?+

99.9% uptime means no more than 8.7 hours of unplanned downtime per year across your managed systems. Our proactive monitoring and rapid response teams maintain this guarantee — and if we miss it, you receive service credits per the terms of your contract.

Do you work with our existing IT team?+

Absolutely. Many of our clients have internal IT staff. We work as an extension of your team, handling the routine, time-consuming tasks like monitoring and patching — freeing your IT team to focus on strategic, high-value initiatives.
